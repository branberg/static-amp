Let’s Make Some Noise

Written by Mike Berg on February 11, 2014

We have loud plans to change the way artists live online. Our goal is to empower musicians with an indispensable toolset, so they can best reach and grow their audience. Amp is a content management system built just for musicians. We’re making Amp because we love music. Seriously. We love to hear it, make it, and share it.

Meet our team:

Mike Berg is the designer and songwriter. He designed this blog and is designing Amp. He also wrote this short bio in third person (awkward).

Sam Brannon is the front-end developer. That’s a fancy term for the guy who transfers designs to scrollable, clickable things on your screen. As you read this, Sam is most likely writing code for Amp while listening to the Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater soundtrack.

Adam Lutz is the back-end programmer and handles all the really heavy-duty code ensuring Amp runs smoothly and correctly. Adam knows how to do very complicated things really well. He is also in a band. Listen to his band.

We’ve been designing and developing websites for musicians for over three years now. In doing so we’ve learned a great deal about their wants and needs. Amp is specifically tailored to fit these wants and needs at an affordable price.

Amp will house:

  • Your Music – Upload and manage your songs, albums, mixtapes, etc. Your fans will be able to stream, share, and download it on your awesome website.
  • Your Shows – Create shows and tours managing individual dates through our flexible tour database.
  • Your Team – Add bandmates, managers, or loyal groupies. Amp allows multiple points of access enabling efficient teamwork behind the scenes.
  • Your Website – No coding necessary. Easily customize your responsive site to suit your needs and goals. You’ll also receive your very own domain name.
  • Your Media – Post photos, sync your Instagram pics, and premiere your music videos like a boss.
  • Your Social Life – Connect with your fans while increasing your audience using Amp’s social integrations.
  • Your Mailing List – Collect email addresses directly from your website and build a loyal following.
  • Your Analytics – Amp provides meaningful and beautiful analytics. Keep track of downloads, site views, number of plays and more.

We are very excited for Amp’s bright future and thankful to those of you who have signed up. If you haven’t signed up, you should. We won’t let you down. Follow us on Twitter and Facebook. We also consistently update our blog with delicious content. We’d love to hear your music and are always taking recommendations.
